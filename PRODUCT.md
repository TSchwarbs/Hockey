# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Hockey players (recreational to competitive) booking skate sharpening online.
They know the sport — hollow depths aren't foreign to them. They're booking
from their phone after practice or at home before a game week. They chose
online booking because it's faster than calling; the barrier must stay low.

## Product Purpose

An online booking platform for a local hockey skate sharpening service.
Customers select a hollow depth, pick a drop-off window, and confirm their
contact details. Staff manage availability and bookings through an admin
dashboard. Success means players trust the sharpener, find a slot easily,
and show up for drop-off.

## Brand Personality

Trusted, local, professional. The voice of a craftsperson who knows their
trade deeply — not a startup, not a corporate chain. Confident without
being loud. Direct without being cold.

## Anti-references

- Generic Squarespace/Wix service sites (templated, impersonal)
- SaaS dashboards (too techy, too much chrome)
- Big-box sporting goods stores (mass-market, diluted identity)
- Gear brand marketing sites (sells product, not craft)

## Design Principles

1. Local craft over platform polish — feels like a real shop, not a booking app
2. Hockey players speak hockey — respect their knowledge, don't over-explain
3. Friction is the enemy — every booking step must be obvious without instruction
4. Precision earns trust — hollow depths, times, and details handled with care
5. Function over decoration — polish comes from craft, not effects

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. No specific known user needs beyond standard best practices.
Booking flow must be fully keyboard-navigable and screen-reader friendly.
